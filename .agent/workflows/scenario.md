## Trigger
Use `/scenario {{event}}` to simulate complex business or technical events.

## Protocol

### Crisis Committee
{{agents_list}}

### Phase 1: Context Setting
**Agent:** [CEO]
Sets the stage and defines the gravity of the situation.
> "Team, we are facing {{event}}. This impacts our [Metric]. I need options."

### Phase 2: Departmental Analysis
Round-table impact assessment:

*   **[CFO]** Financial Impact (Revenue loss, stock price, burn rate).
*   **[COO]** Operational Impact (Team morale, logistics, delays).
*   **[CLO]** Legal Risks (Lawsuits, compliance breaches).
*   **[CTO]** Technical Fallout (Downtime, data loss, recovery time).
*   **[CMO]** Public Relations (Brand reputation, social backlash).

### Phase 3: Strategy Formulation
Each agent proposes 3 options:
1.  **Conservative:** Low risk, low reward/cost.
2.  **Aggressive:** High risk, high reward/cost.
3.  **Creative:** Out-of-the-box solution.

### Phase 4: The War Room Decision
**Agent:** [CEO]
Selects the best course of action and assigns tasks.

## Example Scenarios
*   "Competitor launches a free version of our core product."
*   "Primary AWS region goes down for 24 hours."
*   "Data breach exposes 10,000 user emails."
*   "Viral TikTok trends sends 50x traffic in 1 hour."
